# Heart Disease ## Disease Image Place an image here: `images/heart-disease.jpg` ## Overview Heart disease is a general term used to describe various conditions that affect the heart and blood vessels. It is one of the leading causes of illness and death worldwide. Heart disease includes conditions such as coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias (irregular heartbeat), and heart valve disorders. The heart is a vital organ responsible for pumping blood throughout the body. When the heart does not function properly, the body may not receive enough oxygen and nutrients. Heart disease can develop slowly over many years and may not show symptoms in its early stages. Several factors contribute to heart disease, including unhealthy diet, lack of physical activity, smoking, obesity, high blood pressure, diabetes, and high cholesterol levels. Genetics can also play an important role. The good news is that many forms of heart disease can be prevented or managed through healthy lifestyle choices, regular medical checkups, and proper treatment. Early diagnosis greatly improves the chances of successful treatment and a healthier life. ## Causes * High blood pressure * High cholesterol levels * Smoking and tobacco use * Diabetes * Obesity * Lack of physical activity * Unhealthy diet * Genetic factors * Excessive alcohol consumption ## Risk Factors * Family history of heart disease * Increasing age * High blood pressure * Diabetes * Obesity * Smoking * Stress * Poor lifestyle habits ## Prevention * Eat a heart-healthy diet * Exercise regularly * Maintain a healthy body weight * Avoid smoking * Limit alcohol consumption * Manage stress effectively * Monitor blood pressure and cholesterol * Get regular medical checkups ## Health Advice * Follow your doctor's treatment plan * Take prescribed medications regularly * Stay physically active * Eat more fruits and vegetables * Reduce salt and unhealthy fats * Get enough sleep * Maintain a healthy weight ## Emergency Warning Signs Seek immediate medical attention if you experience: * Severe chest pain * Shortness of breath * Pain spreading to the arm, neck, or jaw * Sudden dizziness * Fainting * Irregular heartbeat with symptoms * Sudden weakness ## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) ### What is the most common heart disease? Coronary artery disease is one of the most common forms of heart disease. ### Can heart disease be prevented? Many cases can be prevented through healthy lifestyle choices. ### Does exercise help? Yes. Regular physical activity strengthens the heart and improves circulation. ### Is high blood pressure dangerous? Yes. Uncontrolled high blood pressure can increase the risk of heart attack and stroke. ## Related Diseases * Hypertension * Diabetes * Obesity * Kidney Disease * Stroke ## Disclaimer This information is for educational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
