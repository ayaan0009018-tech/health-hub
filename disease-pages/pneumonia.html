Pneumonia

Overview

Pneumonia is an infection that inflames the air sacs in the lungs.

Symptoms

  • Cough
  • Fever
  • Chest pain
  • Difficulty breathing

Health Advice

  • Rest adequately
  • Drink fluids
  • Take medicines as prescribed
  • Seek medical help if breathing worsens

# Pneumonia ## Disease Image Place an image here: `images/pneumonia.jpg` ## Overview Pneumonia is a serious infection that affects one or both lungs. The infection causes the air sacs in the lungs, known as alveoli, to become inflamed and fill with fluid or pus. This can make breathing difficult and reduce the amount of oxygen reaching the bloodstream. Pneumonia can be caused by bacteria, viruses, fungi, or other microorganisms. It affects people of all ages but is especially dangerous for infants, young children, older adults, and individuals with weakened immune systems. The severity of pneumonia can range from mild to life-threatening. Common symptoms include cough, fever, chills, chest pain, fatigue, and difficulty breathing. Some patients may produce mucus or phlegm when coughing. In severe cases, hospitalization may be required for oxygen therapy and intensive treatment. Good hygiene practices, vaccination, proper nutrition, and early medical care can significantly reduce the risk of pneumonia and its complications. Prompt treatment is important because untreated pneumonia may lead to respiratory failure, bloodstream infections, or other serious health problems. ## Causes * Bacterial infections * Viral infections * Fungal infections * Influenza virus * COVID-19 infection * Aspiration of food or liquids into the lungs * Weakened immune system ## Risk Factors * Age below 5 years or above 65 years * Smoking * Chronic lung diseases * Heart disease * Diabetes * Weak immune system * Malnutrition * Recent respiratory infections ## Prevention * Get recommended vaccinations * Wash hands regularly * Avoid smoking * Maintain good nutrition * Exercise regularly * Cover your mouth while coughing or sneezing * Seek treatment for respiratory infections promptly ## Health Advice * Take medications exactly as prescribed * Drink plenty of fluids * Get adequate rest * Eat nutritious foods * Monitor breathing symptoms carefully * Avoid smoking and secondhand smoke * Attend follow-up medical appointments ## Emergency Warning Signs Seek immediate medical attention if you experience: * Severe difficulty breathing * Blue lips or fingertips * Persistent high fever * Chest pain while breathing * Confusion or disorientation * Rapid breathing * Severe weakness ## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) ### What is pneumonia? Pneumonia is an infection that causes inflammation of the air sacs in the lungs. ### Is pneumonia contagious? Some forms of pneumonia caused by viruses or bacteria can spread from person to person. ### Can pneumonia be prevented? Yes. Vaccination, good hygiene, and a healthy lifestyle can reduce the risk. ### Who is most at risk? Young children, older adults, and people with weakened immune systems are at higher risk. ## Related Diseases * COVID-19 * Tuberculosis * Influenza * Bronchitis * Asthma ## Disclaimer This information is for educational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
