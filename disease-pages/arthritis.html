# Arthritis ## Disease Image Place an image here: `images/arthritis.jpg` ## Overview Arthritis is a common condition that causes inflammation, pain, stiffness, and swelling in one or more joints. A joint is the place where two bones meet, such as the knees, hips, elbows, shoulders, and fingers. Arthritis can affect people of all ages, but it is more common in older adults. There are more than 100 different types of arthritis. The most common forms are Osteoarthritis and Rheumatoid Arthritis. Osteoarthritis occurs when the protective cartilage that cushions the ends of bones gradually wears away. Rheumatoid Arthritis is an autoimmune disease in which the body's immune system mistakenly attacks the joints. Arthritis can range from mild discomfort to severe disability. Symptoms may develop gradually over time or appear suddenly. If left untreated, arthritis can reduce mobility and affect a person's quality of life. Although arthritis cannot always be cured, proper treatment, physical activity, healthy eating, and regular medical care can help control symptoms and improve joint function. Early diagnosis and treatment are important to prevent long-term joint damage. ## Causes * Aging and wear of joints * Autoimmune disorders * Joint injuries * Genetic factors * Infections affecting joints * Obesity increasing joint pressure ## Risk Factors * Increasing age * Family history of arthritis * Obesity * Previous joint injuries * Autoimmune diseases * Smoking * Lack of physical activity ## Prevention * Maintain a healthy body weight * Exercise regularly * Protect joints from injuries * Follow a healthy diet * Avoid smoking * Manage chronic health conditions * Stay physically active ## Health Advice * Follow medical treatment plans * Perform gentle stretching exercises * Stay active without overstraining joints * Use supportive devices if recommended * Maintain a healthy weight * Apply heat or cold therapy when advised * Attend regular medical checkups ## Emergency Warning Signs Seek immediate medical attention if you experience: * Sudden severe joint pain * High fever with joint swelling * Inability to move a joint * Severe redness around a joint * Sudden joint deformity * Signs of infection ## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) ### What is the most common type of arthritis? Osteoarthritis is one of the most common forms of arthritis worldwide. ### Can arthritis be cured? Some types cannot be completely cured, but symptoms can often be managed effectively. ### Does exercise help arthritis? Yes. Appropriate exercise can improve joint flexibility and reduce stiffness. ### Can obesity worsen arthritis? Yes.
